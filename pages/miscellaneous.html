Some of my favorite websites

  • Cube Lovers: MIT mailing list dedicated to the mathematics of the Rubik's Cube and similar puzzles active from 1980 to 1999
  • cube20.org: Website written by the team who found Rubik's Cube's God's number, displaying the results obtained from their search and explaining how they did it
  • Jaap's Puzzle Page: Huge collection of twisty puzzles with a guide to solve them, as well as some computational data, like number of positions and God's numbers
  • Orfeas' Webpage: My friend Orfeas' website, from which I drew inspiration to create mine
